4.0 out of 5 starsGorgeous Dress Watch
By SteveRRRR on October 9, 2013
Size: NoneColor: Black Verified Purchase
Beautiful dress watch. Thin and very minimalist with only the slim minute and hour hand, silver marker at 12 o'clock and small date (but big enough to read). The strap attachment is unique with a metal link attaching to the leather strap attaching to the watch. Love the all black look with just hints of silver (buckle, crown, 12 o'clock marker). The hands look silver in the picture, but to me they seem more of a dark gray. They are very shiny/reflective and can be seen in a low light environment. Love the Eco-Drive! No batteries, no winding, no worries.

The only reason I'm not giving it 5 stars is the strap. It says genuine leather. It also says "Band-Thailand". Hard to describe what I don't like about it. It's almost like the top piece is not bonded properly to the bottom piece, so it sort of wrinkles a bit when you flex the band, Not a big deal, as long as it lasts. I think given the unique design an aftermarket strap will be hard to come by...

5.0 out of 5 starsWow!
By Btphan on February 2, 2014
Size: NoneColor: Black Verified Purchase
Very modern, sleek and classy design. Just make sure you're not sagging your pants when wearing this watch.

The watch is fairly light, which makes it very comfortable to wear, but if you're used to heavy watches then you may not like this.

The leather strap is very simple, smooth, black leather. No texture or anything like that. Due to the design of the watch, it may be difficult to find an after-market leather strap that will fit.

Overall, it looks very nice and the eco-drive is nice to have as well.

Other things about the watch:
- The dial is black. However, under very, very strong and direct light, or under direct sunlight, you can tell that the center portion of the dial is dark brown and not black like the rest of the dial surface. There is no way that anyone can tell that it's brown unless they have the watch up to about 6 inches of their face AND in direct sunlight. Photos WITH a flash will still show the dial surface as black, so no worries there.
